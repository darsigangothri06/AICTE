Initiatives & Schemes

Rusa

Improve the overall quality of state institutions by ensuring conformity to prescribed norms and standards and adopt accreditation as a mandatory quality assurance framework initiated by the Ministry of HRD.

Vidyanjali

Vidyanjali is an initiative of the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Department of School Education & Literacy to enhance the community and private sector participation in Govt. schools.

SSA Shagun

States and UT’s are funded to implement activities under different components of SSA. Using this portal, States and UT’s will be able to share their progress & fund utilization.
